Dil Da Mamla Gurdas Maan Live in Concert

For one unforgettable night, witness the magic of the legendary Gurdas Maan live at Bharat Mandapam on 28th June.

Is there an age restriction for Dil Da Mamla Gurdas Maan Live in Concert?
Dil Da Mamla Gurdas Maan Live in Concert is strictly for guests aged 3 and above. Valid government-issued photo ID is required for entry. Guests under 3 will not be admitted. The event will be presented in Hindi, Punjabi.
